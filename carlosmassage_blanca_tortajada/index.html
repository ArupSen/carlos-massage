About me

Hello! My name is Carlos Oronoz. I am a qualified and experienced deep tissue masseur. I graduated from the London School of Sports Massage in early 2006 then studied traditional Thai Massage at Wat Po Temple School of massage in Bangkok, Thailand.

My approach is to provide holistic therapy to improve well being. My well equipped practice is based in West London but I can also offer a visiting service lasting from one to one and a half hours.

Treatments include back massage, head massage and full body massage. Take a look around my site and discover how I might help you find sanctuary from the demands of modern life.
